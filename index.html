about me

In May 2018 I received my BA from Colby College, where I majored in Physics and minored in Studio Art. I am now studying at Columbia University in the City of New York through a dual-degree program. This program allows students to receive a well-rounded and rigorous liberal arts education prior to receiving a BS from Columbia in an Engineering discipline. I have chosen to study Computer Science, allowing me to put to use both my artistic abilities and academic strengths.

I have created this portfolio using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and plan to add more content in the near future. For now, please enjoy some of my illustrations curated and created for my brand, MaddyMadeThis.

More of my artwork can be found at my instagram, @maddymadethis_.
